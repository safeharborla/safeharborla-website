Our Mission
To support community members in breaking the cycle of violence, foster growth and leadership through positive mentorship, and promote social change in our institutions.
Our Approach
We believe in the power of community-centered, cross-sector, collaborative programming to enhance the health of the community members we serve and the care we deliver — from acute care through lasting recovery.

About Us

A safe harbor for
every storm.

Safe Harbor HVIP is a grant- and community- funded violence intervention program housed within the Division of Trauma at Harbor-UCLA Medical Center and the Lundquist Institute for Biomedical Innovation, in partnership with Southern California Crossroads.

Through collaboration with our community partners, we provide patient-centered, trauma-informed case management and counseling services for victims of violent injury.

Our work is rooted in the belief that healing is possible, and that communities have the power to transform cycles of violence into cycles of strength.

"Walking alongside survivors from the moment of injury through their longer journey of recovery."
01
Case Management

Personalized, sustained support guiding survivors through recovery, connecting them to resources, services, and a sustainable path forward.

02
Counseling Services

Trauma-informed counseling that acknowledges the full weight of what survivors carry, provided by clinicians with professional and lived experience.

03
Community Engagement

Programs that bridge the institution to the community to build power and trust towards social change.
